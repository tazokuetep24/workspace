\chapter{Introduction}\label{sec:intro}

In this \namecref{sec:intro} the thesis is introduced. \Cref{sec:motivation} presents the motivation for the chosen topic, \ref{sec:aims} describes the aims of the thesis and \ref{sec:structure} outlines the content.

\section{Motivation}\label{sec:motivation}

Since the introduction of deep learning, the field of machine learning has seen a tremendous growth. In particular, large generative models have shown impressive capabilities in various domains, such as natural language processing and computer vision. However, these models often require significant computational resources for inference, which limits their accessibility and applicability in real-world scenarios. Therefore, there is a growing interest in developing resource-efficient inference techniques for large generative models. This thesis aims to explore and evaluate various approaches to achieve resource-efficient inference while maintaining the performance of large generative models.

\section{Aims}\label{sec:aims}

The main aims of this thesis are as follows:
\begin{itemize}
    \item To review and analyze existing techniques for resource-efficient inference in large generative models.
    \item To propose novel methods for improving the efficiency of inference in large generative models.
    \item To evaluate the proposed methods on benchmark datasets and compare their performance with existing techniques.
    \item To provide insights and recommendations for future research in the field of resource-efficient inference in large generative models.
\end{itemize}

\section{Thesis structure}\label{sec:structure}

The thesis is structured as follows: \Cref{sec:theory} provides a theoretical background on large generative models and resource-efficient inference techniques. \Cref{sec:methods} describes the proposed methods for improving inference efficiency. Finally, \Cref{sec:conclusion} summarizes the findings and discusses future research directions.
